Sustainability

Propane is Clean Energy

Choosing propane isn't just smart — it's a cleaner choice for your home and the environment.

  • Propane produces significantly fewer greenhouse gas emissions than other fossil fuels
  • Recognized as a clean fuel under the Clean Air Act of 1990
  • Burns cleaner than gasoline, diesel, and fuel oil
  • Non-toxic and won't contaminate soil or groundwater if spilled
  • Highly efficient — more energy output per BTU than other fuels
  • Approved alternative fuel by the U.S. Department of Energy
  • Versatile — heating, cooking, water heating, generators & more
60%

Propane produces up to 60% fewer smog-producing hydrocarbons than gasoline — making it one of the cleanest burning conventional fuels available today.
